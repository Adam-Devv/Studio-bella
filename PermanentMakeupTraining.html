Permanent Makeup School

Studio Bella Permanent Makeup has been trainining students four times a year for the past ten years.

Requests have been building and now we're proud to announce that have the space to accomodate an ongoing school fulltime. Our next class will be from April 22 - 26th.

Class Info

Classes are completed within one week. Monday - Friday 10AM - 6PM with a 1 hour lunch break. We also offer weekend classes by request to accomodate those with regular jobs, which take place over 2 consecutive weekends. On the final day you will recieve your final test and be given your Certification as well as a starter kit. Live Models will be provided.

The Teacher

Cookie Bandel is the leader in permanent makeup because she listens then does what clients want. Clients are a part of the whole experience, so there are no unwanted surprises. She works with surgeons, providing her services to cancer survivors with areola repigmentation. That's one way she gives back to her community. She offers classes to share her knowledge and passion of permanent makeup. She has been recognized for correcting and camouflaging others' permanent makeup and teaching the next generaion perfect, lasting permanent cosmetics. Regardsless of whether clients choose Cookie, she is happy to share what to ask and look for in a permanent-makeup artist. Featured in Austin Woman Magazine multiple times, she is a trusted and knowledgeable teacher with over a decade of experience in her craft.

Pricing

For the full training course the price is $4000 all-inclusive. One price covers it all, with no hidden fees, students are never surprised with additional charges and can plan their expenses accordingly.

Students who are interested will also be provided with one free Permanent Makeup Procedure by Cookie, including 2 free touch-ups, valued at $650!

Upon completion students will recieve a Professional Certification, and a kit which includes: Quality Tattoo Machine, Needles, Starter Ink Set, Case, Numbing Cream, Bibs, Consent form templates and other supporting documents.

Refunds/Cancellations

We understand plans do not always work out as intended and emergencies arise, and so we offer full refunds up to 2 weeks prior to class start date, and a 50% refund if between 1-2 weeks before class. No refunds will be given within 1 week of class start date as that is too little time to find a student to fill your spot.
