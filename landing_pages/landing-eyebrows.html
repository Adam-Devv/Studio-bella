Eyebrow Techniques at Studio Bella

Cookie Bandel, a master technician since 2008, offers several eyebrow techniques to achieve your desired look. Whether you want the natural effect of hair strokes, the soft definition of a powder brow, or a combination, we customize the procedure for you. We use two or three different shades to give your eyebrows dimension, especially beneficial for those with sparse or no hair. All procedures include a consultation to discuss shape and color.

Hair Strokes (Microblading)

Creates fine, hair-like lines for the most natural, undetectable look.

Powder Effect (Ombre/Shading)

Gives a soft, filled-in makeup look, similar to using brow powder.

Combination Brows

Uses both hair strokes (usually at the front) and shading for added definition and fullness.
