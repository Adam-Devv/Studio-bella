Permanent Makeup Training

Take your PMU career to the next level with our 5 Day all inclusive Permanent Makeup course. This course covers all the latest PMU techniques including lips, eyeliner, and eyebrows. Benefit from our generous kits with our state of the art permanent makeup machine and couture medical grade pigments. Work on live models in class with the assistance and guidance of our experienced trainers. Learn techniques to offer your clients 8 different services after class - Lip liner, full lips, nano brows, ombre brow, full shaded brow, eyeliner, full lash affect and corrective treatments.

Course Includes:

Permanent Makeup Starter Kit

Kit with a PMU machine, needle cartridges, anesthetic, and more, everything you need to begin working on clients.

Business Training

A comprehensive overview of business ethics, forms, and regulations relevant to the industry so you can hit the ground running.

Trusted Instructors

Certified and well-reviewed instructors to guide you every step of the way.

Live Models

Work on live models with supervision and guidance, as well as observe Instructors working on clients in a teaching setting.
