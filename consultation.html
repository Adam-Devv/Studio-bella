Why Book a Consultation?

  • Get personalized advice tailored to your unique features & goals.
  • See before/after photos and discuss your desired look.
  • Ask all your questions about the process, safety, and aftercare (see FAQ).
  • Discuss color, shape, and technique options (e.g., microblading vs. powder brows).
  • Meet Cookie and feel comfortable before committing.
  • Understand the procedure and what to expect during healing.
  • Absolutely free with no obligation to book a service.

What to Expect During Your Consultation

Your free consultation at Studio Bella is designed to be informative and comfortable, typically lasting 15-30 minutes.

Discussion & Goal Setting

We'll chat about the specific services you're interested in (eyebrows, eyeliner, lips, paramedical, etc.) and the aesthetic you hope to achieve. Feel free to bring inspiration photos!

Education & Techniques

Cookie will explain the different methods available (like microblading vs. powder brows, lip blush vs. liner), the procedure steps, healing process, and realistic outcomes.

Portfolio Review

You'll have the chance to look through extensive photo albums of Cookie's actual client results, helping you visualize the possibilities for your own transformation.

Pre-Drawing (Optional)

For services like eyebrows or lips, we can optionally perform a temporary pre-draw with conventional makeup. This gives you a preview of the proposed shape before committing to a procedure appointment.

Q&A Session

This is your dedicated time to ask any questions you have about the procedure, longevity, safety, cost, comfort, or anything else on your mind.

Assessment (If Applicable)

For certain procedures, Cookie might assess your skin type or the specific area to be treated to determine the most suitable technique and pigment choice. We can also discuss color matching.

In-Person or Virtual

Consultations can be conveniently held either **in person** at our beautiful Austin studio or **virtually via Zoom**, whichever suits you best.

No Pressure

Our goal is purely informational. We want you to feel fully confident and comfortable before deciding to proceed with any service. There's absolutely no obligation.
