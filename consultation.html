What to Expect During Your Consultation

Your free consultation at Studio Bella is designed to be informative and comfortable. Master Technician Cookie Bandel will dedicate approximately 15-30 minutes to personally discuss your permanent makeup goals.

Here's what typically happens:

  • Discussion: We'll talk about the specific areas you're interested in (eyebrows, eyeliner, lips, paramedical, etc.) and the look you hope to achieve. Feel free to bring inspiration photos!
  • Education: Cookie will explain the different techniques available (like microblading vs. powder brows, lip blush vs. liner), the process itself, and what realistic results look like.
  • Portfolio Review: You'll have the opportunity to look through extensive photo albums of Cookie's actual client work, helping you visualize the possibilities.
  • Q&A: This is your time to ask any questions you have about the procedure, healing, longevity, safety, or anything else on your mind.
  • Assessment (Optional): For some procedures, Cookie might assess your skin type or the area to be treated to recommend the best approach. We can also discuss color matching.
  • Pre-Drawing (Optional): For services like eyebrows or lips, we can optionally do a temporary pre-draw with makeup to give you an idea of the proposed shape before you commit to a procedure appointment.

Consultations can be held either **in person** at our Austin studio or **virtually via Zoom** for your convenience. Our goal is to ensure you feel fully informed and confident before moving forward with any permanent makeup service.
