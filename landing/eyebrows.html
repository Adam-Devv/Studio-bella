Choosing Your Perfect Eyebrow Technique

Cookie Bandel, a master technician since 2008, expertly tailors permanent eyebrow techniques to your unique features and desired outcome. We use multiple shades for natural dimension and always include a thorough consultation to perfect your shape and color.

Microblading (Hair Strokes)

Ideal for achieving the most natural look, microblading involves creating fine, crisp strokes that mimic individual eyebrow hairs. This technique is perfect for adding fullness to sparse brows or creating a subtle enhancement.

  • Best For: Normal to dry skin types, clients wanting a very natural, 'undetectable' enhancement.
  • Look: Realistic, individual hair strokes.
  • Benefit: Mimics natural hair growth pattern seamlessly.

Powder, Ombre & Combination Brows

These techniques utilize machine shading to create a soft, filled-in appearance, similar to using brow powder or pencil. They offer more definition and are excellent options for various skin types.

  • Powder/Ombre Brows: Creates a soft, pixelated effect, often lighter at the front and gradually darkening towards the tail (Ombre). Ideal for those who prefer a defined, 'makeup' look or have oily skin.
  • Combination Brows: The best of both worlds! Combines microbladed hair strokes (usually at the front) with machine shading through the body and tail for natural texture plus definition. A very popular choice.
  • Best For: All skin types (especially oily), clients seeking defined shape, covering old PMU.
