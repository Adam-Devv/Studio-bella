PMU Correction & Removal Services

It's disheartening when permanent makeup doesn't turn out as expected. Common issues include incorrect color (e.g., brows turning grey or orange), unnatural shapes, asymmetry, or pigment migration. Cookie Bandel is highly skilled and experienced in addressing these problems with precision and care.

Saline Pigment Removal/Lightening

A safe and effective method using a specialized saline solution to lift unwanted pigment from the skin. Often requires multiple sessions. Ideal for lightening brows or small areas before correction.

Color Correction & Shape Adjustment

Utilizing advanced color theory and techniques to neutralize undesirable tones (like blue, grey, red, orange) and carefully adjust the shape of existing PMU for a more flattering and natural look.

Important: A thorough consultation is mandatory for all correction/removal work. Cookie needs to assess the existing pigment, your skin type, and discuss realistic outcomes and the potential number of sessions required. Your safety and satisfaction are the top priorities.
