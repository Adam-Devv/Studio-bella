Meet Cookie Bandel: Master Technician & Founder

Cookie Bandel, founder of Studio Bella, has been a dedicated Master Permanent Makeup Technician since 2008. As a well-established and respected member of the Austin cosmetic tattoo community, Cookie combines artistic talent with technical precision to create beautiful, natural-looking results tailored to each client.

Her expertise has been recognized by features in KVUE Austin and Austin Woman Magazine. Known for her meticulous work and commitment to safety, Cookie is often sought after for complex procedures, including permanent makeup corrections and restorative paramedical tattooing like Areola Repigmentation. Clients trust Cookie's skill and compassionate approach, often travelling from other cities and states for her services.

Our Services & Philosophy

Studio Bella specializes in a wide range of permanent cosmetic services designed to enhance your natural beauty and simplify your daily routine. Our most popular procedures include:

  • Permanent Eyebrows: Microblading, Powder Brows, and Combination techniques for perfectly shaped, natural-looking brows.
  • Permanent Eyeliner: From subtle lash enhancement to defined liner, always smudge-proof.
  • Permanent Lip Color: Define shape and boost natural color with Lip Blush or liner.
  • Corrections & Removal: Expert solutions for unsatisfactory permanent makeup done elsewhere.
  • Paramedical Procedures: Including Areola Restoration (free for breast cancer survivors) and Scar Camouflage.

Our philosophy centers on collaboration and client satisfaction. We believe in thorough consultations (always free!) to understand your goals, discuss options, and ensure you feel confident and informed. We prioritize using high-quality pigments and adhering to the strictest safety and sterilization standards.
