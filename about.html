About Studio Bella

Cookie Bandel has been practicing Permanent Makeup since 2008 and is a well-established member of the Austin Tattoo Community and is featured on KVUE Austin and in Austin Woman Magazine. From makeup repairs to aereola repigmentation, she is well known as one of the top professionals in Texas, with clients travelling from other cities and states to get her services.

Originally in a smaller location located on City Park Road known as the "little yellow house", she has since opened Studio Bella Spa which houses a 3,000 square foot Boutique, a full-featured permanent makeup training course, and numerous other beauty professionals.
