What is Permanent Cosmetic Makeup?

Permanent cosmetic makeup, also known as micropigmentation or cosmetic tattooing, involves implanting colored pigment into the upper layer of the skin (dermis). This creates long-lasting enhancements that mimic traditional makeup.

Procedures utilize specialized tools, including digital machines or manual methods (like microblading). The process starts with a thorough free consultation to discuss your goals, followed by pigment application, and typically includes a follow-up visit to perfect the results.

Studio Bella offers a wide range of services including Eyebrows (Microblading & Powder Brows), Eyeliner (Classic & Lash Enhancement), Lips (Liner & Lip Blush), Beauty Marks, Corrections/Removal, and specialized Paramedical Procedures like Areola Restoration and Scar Camouflage.
